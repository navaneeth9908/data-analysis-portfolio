# FY2024 Analytics Board Update

## Executive summary
The analytics team ended the quarter with stronger self-serve reporting adoption and better pipeline reliability. West-region expansion accounts contributed the largest incremental revenue, while enterprise renewals need closer coordination with procurement and security stakeholders.

## Revenue highlights
ARR rose 18% year over year after expansion in the West region. Healthcare customers adopted the analytics starter package quickly, and services attach rate improved after dashboard enablement workshops.

## Risk watch
Enterprise renewal approvals were delayed because a security review cycle took longer than planned. The account team needs a procurement checklist before the next steering committee, plus a named executive sponsor for each renewal over 100,000 dollars.

## Data operations
Pipeline incident volume fell from seven incidents to three incidents after stricter data-quality gates were added to the weekly load. The remaining incidents came from late-arriving CRM exports and inconsistent customer segment labels.

## Next actions
Sales operations will publish a weekly renewal-risk tracker with owner, blocker, and next meeting date. Data engineering will add a segment-label validation rule before the monthly executive dashboard refresh.
